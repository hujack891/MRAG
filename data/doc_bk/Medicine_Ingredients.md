# Medicine Ingredients

Medicine Ingredients are one of the most plentiful resources in Black Myth: Wukong. The game is filled with them across the different areas and chapters They are vital for crafting new Consumable Items or stockpiling them to give you an edge in boss battles and tough fights. 

In addition to ingredients, you can also find formulas to craft new consumable items and seeds to grow materials. Both of these types of Medicine Ingredients are rarer, so you will have to hunt them down using this guide and our walkthrough.
